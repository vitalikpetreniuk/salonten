Beauty

Treatments for hands and feet

Salon Ten uses the finest Jessica range of nail products, to help create and maintain beautiful nails.

Jessica luxury manicure

Everyone’s nails are different, a treatment manicure which analyses and treats each nail for its specific nail type. The cuticles are cared for with Jessica technology, oils and creams. The nail shape is perfected and basecoats and colour are applied for the perfect finish.

Jessica deluxe manicure

A luxury manicure along with thermal mittens for a deeper penetration of oils and creams, softening cuticles, improving circulation and soothing stiff joints.

Jessica luxury spa pedicure

The ultimate treat for your feet, using the aromatherapy based products which analyses and treats the nail. The foot is soaked in oils and cared for with Jessica technology. The nails are perfected and basecoats and colour are applied for a perfect finish.

Jessica deluxe spa pedicure

A luxury pedicure along with thermal booties for a deeper penetration of oils and creams.

Callus peel

An amazing foot treatment especially for that stubborn hard callused skin. Your foot is transformed after just one treatment removing your callus just like orange peel

File and paint

No frills just a quick tidy and application of polish!

Jessica 'soak off' soft gels

This revolutionary gel takes nails to new dimensions, with stunning results. Jessica Soak Off can be applied and removed in a snap. Formulated with perfect viscosity, crystal clear clarity and a glossy finish. Easy to use and also easy on the natural nail as well, ensuring that no damage is done to the underlying nail bed.

Salon ten's glamour nails

Using the same technique as our Jessica ‘Soak Off’ Soft Gels but with extra glamour! Our Salon Ten glamour nails substitutes the use of a coloured gel with pure glitter encased within layers of clear gel. Your nails will shine and sparkle for up to 2 -3 weeks!
