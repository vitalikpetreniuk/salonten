Hair extensions

Gold class

Gold Class hair extensions are the creation of multi-award winning Inanch Emir, a leading British hairdresser for over 25 years, who is renowned, globally, for her knowledge and unique application techniques with hair extensions.

Inanch has worked with world’s leading hair extension brands and now has a huge celebrity following as well as clientele from the world of fashion and beauty.

There are only ten salons in the UK that offer Gold Class hair extensions in addition to Inanch’s prestigious salon on London’s Great Portland Street and Salon Ten is proud to be one of those salons!

Inanch has stated: “I wanted to create a luxury hair extensions brand sourcing the most premium quality hair and combine this with an accredited application technique with aftercare treatments to give my clients the assurance that their hair extensions will look completely natural and at the same time be totally kind to their own hair leaving no damage after removal. I’m proud that we have achieved all of this with Gold Class”.

Gold Class hair extensions are 100% remi human hair and are gently processed by hand to create the finest quality hair extensions.

Remi hair is considered to be the premium quality of human hair because the cuticles are kept intact. By preserving the hairs’ cuticles and aligning them in the same direction ensures that the hair remains super healthy and tangle-free. Gold Class hair extensions are also ‘double drawn’ which means the thickness of the strands are the same at the top as they are at the bottom.

Gold Class application techniques ensure that the hair extension bonds are virtually undetectable and extremely comfortable. The polymer bonds have the same characteristics as your own hair and scalp, giving a natural look and feel. They are also mouldable, so can be formed into either a flat or round shape.

Our Gold Class specialists are fully trained to assess exactly how many bonds you will need before your new look comes to life with a great new cut, colour or style!

Color options

Gold Class hair extensions are available in a range of 26 natural shades to offer the perfect colour match to your own hair.

Gold Class offer a range of aftercare products to keep your hair in great condition and all are available at the salon or on line.
