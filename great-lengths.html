Hair extensions

Great lengths

Great Lengths has been established for over 25 years and can now be found in selected salons across the world. Every strand of Great Lengths hair is traceable and ethically sourced, guaranteeing the origin and quality of their hair extensions.

Great Lengths hair extensions are 100% human hair, so can be treated in exactly the same way as your own hair –straightened, curled, permed and coloured and are suitable for all hair types.

Salon Ten is accredited as a Gold status salon and has been working with Great Lengths for many years. This accolade ensures that you will be looked after by an experienced and certified stylist who will, not only create the look and colour that you desire, but will offer advice and support to keep your hair in the finest condition.

Great Lengths hair extensions can be applied all over your head to give length and volume to your current hairstyle, or added to a specific area to enhance your natural look. They can be attached with individual bonds for a longer lasting style of up to five months, or with tape for a more temporary look, lasting only a few weeks, that can also be reused.

Great Lengths hair extensions will not cause damage to healthy hair when applied correctly and are well maintained. We will give you advice on the type of products to be used and the best way to look after your hair.
