Wigs

Headwear and headscarves

As an alternative to wearing a wig, Salon Ten has a huge choice of comfortable and stylish headwear for those who are suffering hair loss. With plenty of styles in stock (and at our online shop) you are welcome to come and try various styles in our discreet wig floor. If we haven’t got the one you want in a particular colour, then we can order it for you. Collections change throughout the year and we have Spring/Summer and Autumn/Winter variations – whatever the time of year or your mood/style, we will have something to suit.

Wig care routine

Christine Headwear is a range of Danish designed headwear products made to provide women with a real choice in hair coverings, and the opportunity to feel beautiful during a difficult time.

The Company was founded in 2003 by Tine Fuglsang when she discovered how difficult it was for women, who suffer from hair loss, to find soft, comfortable and fashionable alternatives to wigs and old-fashioned scarves.

Little or no hair exposes the scalp, making it vulnerable to weather conditions and the harmful radiation from the sun. With two annual collections, spring/summer and autumn/winter, we are able to follow changing trends and give women the opportunity to find the perfect headwear to fit any wardrobe all year round. Our large range of headwear styles includes fitted scarves, hats, turbans, sleep caps, headbands, knitwear and sun wear.

All hair covering products are made from natural materials such as bamboo viscose, wool and cotton, because a very sensitive scalp without hair needs special and comfortable protection. These natural fibres ensure that all the products are breathable and moisture absorbent.

All Christine products are made without irritating seams, as seams are hidden under a soft bamboo lining. Our wig liners are completely seamless for extra comfort. Furthermore, all Christine Headwear products are CE Labelled, meaning that they are classified as medical devices according to EU Directive (93/42/EEC) concerning medical devices.
