Body treatments

Customised escapes

This 100% customised body treatment allows you to tailor make your treatment to suit your mood and needs. You choose from three massage products and one scrub with unique textures combined with one of three fragrance options: Lemon & Petitgrain, Orange Blossom & Cedar Wood or Vanilla & Sandalwood.

Customised full body

A full body treatment including exfoliation and massage leaving your skin soft and smooth.

Customised exfoliation

Invigorating exfoliation combining sea salts and sugars and customised with a fragrance of your choice.

Customised massage

A full body massage tailor made for you.

The event escape

This limited edition body treatment uses exfoliating and massage syrups to create a unique sensorial escape with a new delicious fragrance each year. The relaxing scrub eliminates dead skin cells leaving skin soft, smooth and hydrated and a comforting massage product is used that provides a warming effect that instantly melts on contact with the skin. Your body will be left with a delicate fragrance that you will smell for the rest of the day.

Hanakasumi massage

This Japanese inspired massage begins with cleansing of the skin to remove impurities, then a combination of massage and reflexology revive energy flows and circulation. All of this combined with the cherry blossom fragrance makes Hanakasumi the ultimate treatment for relaxation.

Orient essences ceremony

Based on oriental traditions, the journey starts with a scrub using spices, followed by a nourishing massage enriched with aromatic bags and warm stones. This rejuvenating and relaxing ritual is guaranteed to transport you to another world.

Peace of the senses

Full body Swedish body massage.

Back massage

A wonderful back massage using the finest oils for that ultimate relaxing experience enjoyed equally by men and women alike a very popular treatment.

Lava shell massage

This revolutionary and unique massage for women and men uses naturally self-warming tiger clam shells to deliver a seamless treatment tailored to suit your individual needs. The warm smooth shells are expertly glided over the body with varying pressures, techniques and precision enabling the treatment to moisturise the skin and the warming energy of the shells helps to revitalise and rebalance the body and mind.

Mini lava/detox shell tummy treatment

With a choice of either Lava or Detox shell, the combination of soothing heat or detoxifying coolness and gentle massage around the abdominal area stimulates the body natural systems to effectively eliminate toxins and reduce bloating, leaving you with a flatter tummy for days.

Please confirm which type of massage you would like, on booking, so we can prepare the shells for you.

Glacial shells

Glacial shells are outstanding when combined with lava shell. The combination of the hot and cold treatment on sensory points aids the breakdown of fatty tissues, stimulates a sluggish circulation and moves uncomfortable build up in the digestive system.

Indian head massage

The aim of Indian head massage is to release the stress that has accumulated in the tissues, muscles and joints of the head, face, neck and shoulders.

Hopi ear candles

An ancient and natural therapy handed down by many civilisations. Why not add to a facial or massage to leave you feeling totally refreshed?
