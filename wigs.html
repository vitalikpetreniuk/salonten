Wigs

Wigs

There are many reasons why men and women choose to wear a wig.

Whether the choice is due to medically related hair loss or simply because you fancy a change from your normal everyday hair style, at Salon Ten we have over 200 wigs, always in stock, which can be worn on a daily, or occasional, basis.

On our dedicated and private wig floor, our specially trained wig experts can offer advice and support on the huge range of wig styles and colours to choose from, both human hair and synthetic hair – so you will be able to make an informed choice on the right wig for you.

Your chosen wig can be ‘ready to wear’ or be cut and styled to ensure that you have exactly the look you want.

Whatever your motives, knowing the options that are available to you is the first step to making a wise decision.

Why not give us a call on 01952 727555 and book an appointment?
