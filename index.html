Welcome to the award winning salon ten

Shropshire’s award winning hair, beauty & wig salon

Set within a stunning Grade II listed malt house in the beautiful town of Much Wenlock, we have three dedicated hair, beauty and wig floors. We offer a complete range of hair styling, hair extensions, beauty treatments, wigs and hair enhancement services all within an eco-friendly and sustainable environment.
