Products

Products

We are the only salon in Shropshire to use and sell Davines cruelty-free vegan products. Davines is a unique company in the industry that cares about the environment, investing back into sustainable projects throughout the world. Here at Salon Ten we love the look, feel and finish that we are able to obtain using these unique products. You can maintain these looks between visits as we sell everything you need to keep that special look - many in refillable non-plastic bottles of course!

Davines naturaltech energizing range

Does your scalp need a pick me up? Do you want to stop thinning hair in its tracks? Then you need to meet Davines’ Energizing range, a must have for thinning or breakage prone hair.

The Energizing range is formulated with caffeine phytoceuticals, this hard-working formula stimulates circulation and improves elasticity, preventing further or future hair loss with continued use.

Benefitting from eucalyptus and peppermint oil extracts as well as niacinamide and citric acid, this hard-working shampoo works to cleanse, clear and calm irritated and congested scalps.

Davines nou nou hair range

This creamy, nourishing Davines Nou Nou range is designed to cleanse and care for dry, stressed or colour-treated hair. This gentle formula helps to maintain the vibrancy of your colour and illuminate your hair’s subtleties of tone.

The formula deeply conditions your hair whilst providing protection from environmental factors, strengthening your hair and leaving you with glossy locks that are full of life.

The Nou Nou Hair Mask is ideal as a deep conditioning treatment for blondes and those who have damaged their hair with heated styling tools. The mask nourishes your hair without weighing it down.

Davines minu hair range

The Davines Minu range is the ideal hair care system for coloured hair.

The Minu formula is designed to deeply nourish coloured hair, make it soft and silky, extending the duration of professional hair colour, without weighing the hair down.

Your hair will feel replenished, and results in luminous hair colour. The natural active ingredients in the range are designed to deeply hydrate your hair, leaving it feeling silky and easier to manage.

For a weekly treat, use the nourishing Minu Mask to nourish your coloured hair and extend the life of your hair colour’s vibrancy.

Davines oi hair care

This award winning range of hair care products work as part of a daily routine and to help style hair.

Davines OI Hair Butter is our favourite hair moisturiser and is suitable for all hair types!

This luxurious hair butter adds tons of shine, will meet all your hydration needs and even has an anti frizz effect.

The OI Oil combats frizz, promotes shine and protects from styling and environmental damage.

This antioxidant nourishing range provides hair with an extraordinary softness and radiance Not only that, but they smell delicious!

Davines love range for curly hair

The award winning Davines LOVE hair care range was designed specifically for wavy and curly hair. The products work to combat frizz, creating soft and bouncy curls with definition.

Targeting individual curls to revitalise and soften, LOVE products are rich in proteins and vitamins that are renowned for their ability to replenish and restore elasticity and volume to dry, limp locks.

The Davines LOVE shampoo gently cleanses the hair whilst the conditioner deeply nourishes and controls the structure of your curls, without weighing them down.

The Davines LOVE styling range includes a leave in curl cream to smooth and define your beautiful curls and protect from humidity.

Davines replumping range

Is your hair prone to breakage after too many treatments, bleaching and heat application? Then you need to experience Davines’ Natural Tech Replumping range.

This creamy, deliciously fragranced formula is bursting with hair-loving active ingredients to help enliven dry and brittle strands, deeply hydrate and encourage elasticity within hair for stronger, thicker and fuller locks that you’ll adore.

Find out more about this range by speaking to a McGills expert and purchase online via our store.
